Leomon is a leonine Beast Man Digimon armed with a blade and master of the "Fist of the Beast King" attack. Before Bio-Emerging to the Real World, Leomon only knew of endless conflict and was at first a bit frightened and confused by Jeri's affection and insistence that he should become her partner. He eventually grew to care for her, becoming Jeri's partner for real after his near-death experience with Zhuqiaomon during his attempt to halt Makuramon's escape into the Digital World with Calumon
